LU BA Comm Graduates to Present Paper at AMIC 2025 in India

𝗯𝘆: Donjie Limjoco

Aliah C. Centilles, Edenne Jezael Bosque, and Lorenz Gabriel M. Collantes, with adviser Ms. Audrey Lou Dimasaca, will present their thesis “Gendered Marketing: Influence of Gender Roles in Selected Philippine Advertisements,” aligning with the conference theme “Navigating Digital Transformation in Media, Public Relations, and Advertising.”

This marks the second consecutive year that LU’s BA Communication program will be represented at AMIC. In 2024, Ms. Dimasaca and alumna Coleen Garcia presented their study in Beijing, China.
